ASIA

1.Philippines

Boracay

Experience the most beautiful beaches here in the Philippines, with a long stretch of powdery white sand, crystal clear and azure waters, and a stunning sunset.If you want to relax and enjoy, you should visit this place.

2.China

Great Wall Of China

Experience one of the iconic symbol of china. It is an incredible feat of engineering of China and the greatest hand made building structure may ever build by mankind in ancient time. That's why this made Great Wall is a greatest ancient tourist attraction and will continue to provide breathtaking view and awesome sceneries all round the seasons.

3.Japan

Mount Fuji

If you love climbing mountains and thrilling, challenging adventure you must visit the iconic mountain in Japan. As Japan's highest peak, scaling Mount Fuji offers the perfect opportunity to witness breathtaking panoramic views and immerse oneself in nature's grandeur.

EUROPE

1.France

Eiffel Tower

Experience and feel the romantic place and most visited place in the world. The Eiffel Tower with its classic facade, and amazing views from the observation decks inside, and to see its dazzling nightly light shows. It's also the focal point of many important events, including Paris' Bastille Day and New Year's fireworks celebrations.

2.Italy

Colosseum

Come and discover the history of the Largest Amphitheatre in the World. It's an imposing construction that, with almost 2,000 years of history, will bring you back in time to discover the way of life in the Roman Empire.

3.Spain

La Sagrada Familia

If you are planning a trip to Barcelona in Spain, you should not miss your chance to visit the Sagrada Familia. From the glittering stained glass windows to the towering columns and arches to the intricate details in the architecture, every aspect of this incredible church will take your breath away.
